The intermediate track is for the options trader who has learned the basics and is ready to start finding and trading high probability options strategies.

Summary

For the options trader who knows the basics and has made a handful of real trades, this course is designed to get you from being simply "aware" of the concepts to being able to scan for setups, choose the right options strategy, and correctly place high probability trades. Even if you have traded options for years but have yet to find consistent success, we highly suggest you complete this curriculum before moving the Advanced section. Don't let the name fool you, this Intermediate course is full of very advanced topics that the vast majority of traders never fully understand.

Objectives

  • Understand the major difference between historical and implied volatility.
  • Scaling position size and targeting overall portfolio returns.
  • Scanning for the best stock setups and choosing the right options strategy.
  • How to choose strikes prices, expiration dates and fill pricing.
  • Placing contingent order types to automate the entry/exit process.
