Portfolio Management

To properly manage your portfolio, you need to know how to monitor your positions, determine proper allocation size, and reduce margin requirements.

Summary

When I say "portfolio management," some people automatically assume you need a Masters from MIT to understand the concept and strategies - that is NOT the case. In this module you'll see why managing your risk when trading is actually quite simple. Of course, you do need to use simple checks and balances to help remove your emotions and make your trading more systematic.

Objectives

  • Quick pep talk on the importance of patience and letting the math play out.
  • Scaling or adjusting position orders and contract numbers to keep positions sizes small.
  • Monitoring positions and creating automatic alerts for key adjustment levels.
  • Beta-weighting your portfolio and using one benchmark index for determining balance.
